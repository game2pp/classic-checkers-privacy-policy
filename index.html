Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Classic Checkers Board (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Classic Checkers Board does not collect, store, or share personal information. We do not request or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location data, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information.

The game can be played offline. Any game-related data (such as settings, scores, or progress) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. We do not share data with third parties.

3. Advertising

Classic Checkers Board does not display advertisements. There are no banner ads, interstitial ads, or rewarded ads.

4. Children’s Privacy

This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected, children can use the game safely.

5. Data Security

We do not transmit personal data to any server. Any app data remains on your device. While no digital system can be guaranteed 100% secure, the absence of data collection greatly reduces privacy risk.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
